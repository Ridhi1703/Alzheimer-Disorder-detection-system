Models and Diagnostic Approach

This section details the MRI and Clinical models used for Alzheimer's prediction. These models, when combined, provide a robust and multimodal approach to early detection, leveraging neural networks for image processing and clinical insights for enhanced accuracy.

Model Architecture and Workflow

Clinical Model: Analyzes demographic, cognitive, and test data.

MRI Model: Classifies images into four categories (Non-Demented, Mild Demented, Moderate Demented, Severe Demented) using CNNs.

Integration: Combines outputs via multimodal fusion with an accuracy of 84.3%.
